RADWIMPS
Formed in 2001, Japanese 4-piece band RADWIMPS debuted on major labels in 2005.
They quickly attracted a large following, with the younger generation constituting
the majority. The group won awards for Best Original Score at the Japanese Academy
Awards for their compositions for the soundtracks to the animated feature films
"Your Name" and "Weathering with You," which broke records. Their musical endeavors
extend well beyond Japan; they tour all over the world. Nov. 2021 saw the band primarily
release their most recent studio album.

The album “FOREVER DAZE” and embarked on a nationwide tour to support the album.
It was written and recorded throughout the pandemic and speaks to the musical
challenges the guys faced, and their hopes moving forward and features a diverse
group of collaborators and experimental elements.
